The Best 3 Movies Acording to Angela

My favourite movies all times

The Shawshank Redemption (1994):

Genre: Drama
Synopsis: Adapted from a Stephen King novella, this film follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for a crime he didn't commit. The film explores themes of hope, friendship, and redemption as Andy befriends fellow inmates and seeks a sense of freedom within the confines of the prison.

The Godfather (1972):

Genre: Crime, Drama
Synopsis: Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, "The Godfather" is a classic crime film based on Mario Puzo's novel. The story revolves around the powerful Italian-American Mafia family led by patriarch Vito Corleone. It delves into themes of power, family, and the consequences of a life of organized crime.

Inception (2010):

Genre: Sci-Fi, Action
Synopsis: Directed by Christopher Nolan, "Inception" is a mind-bending heist film that explores the concept of dreams within dreams. Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, is a skilled thief who specializes in entering people's dreams to steal their deepest secrets. The film delves into complex layers of reality and the nature of perception, blurring the lines between dreams and reality.
